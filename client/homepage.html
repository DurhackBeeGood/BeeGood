What is BeGood. ?

More than just a Bee Fan page

BeeTogether is a social media based charity initiative to encourage volounteering and giving in a productive and effecient way. Make a profile and find charities in you local area to join. Getting more points or 'buzz' as we call it which goes towards planting trees and money with our partner charities.

Partnering Organisations.

We partner and assess potential charities, then match them up with a users profiles, give money to charities as a one off or on a regular basis and volunteer with charities in your vicinity.

Get Started...

Sign Up as an organisation to get people in your local area to hear about your charity. Or as an individual and create a charity portfolio of all your charities that you send money to and earn 'buzz' points for each hour of volunteering, get to certain milestones and trees will be planted for you, and money will be matched by our sponsers.
